How to Play

X and O take turns tapping empty cells on the 3×3 board. Place your mark — whoever gets 3 in a row first wins!

🏆

Winning Condition

Get 3 of your marks in a row — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. Fill all cells with no winner? It's a draw!

📊

Stats & Profile

When signed in, your Wins, Losses and Draws are saved. Check your Profile tab to view your history and rank.

🔄
